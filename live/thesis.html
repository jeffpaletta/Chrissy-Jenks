Bachelor's Thesis

Enchanted

Concept Statement

I am a dreamer who strongly believes in hope and capturing the beauty in everything. I believe in romance, whimsy, and concept within a photograph. I strive to show this hope and beauty within the images that I create, and I wish to inspire others to see the loveliness that drives my passion to photograph what I do. My approach to photography is based on this firm belief that I carry with me through each day in life, finding inspiration from every moment and emotion that I experience.

Photography is my vessel for expressing these stories that I want to share. I have always been a reader and an avid lover of all things fantasy, fairytale, and make believe. I dream of creating impossible worlds full of wonder and imagination, hoping my audience will fall headfirst into them and lose themselves in enchantment. Through- out my life, I have been fortunate enough to know many strong women who have each greatly influenced me in their own way. With my photographs, I wish to become a storyteller myself in order to share the inspiration that these women have impressed upon me.

Combining fashion, concept, and fabrication, I am creating
imaginative narratives in which each image becomes a window
into a world of it’s own.
